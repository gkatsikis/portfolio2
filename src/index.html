Viaggio: Be Inspired to Explore

Viaggio is a Full CRUD MERN app that was completed in a team using a feature branch workflow. It allows users to post destinations that they have visited, add destinations to a bucket list, and add comments to other's posts. Viaggio is intended to be a place where users can look at past destinations travelers have been to and be inspired to plan a trip of their own.
